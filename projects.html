Projects

The poster projects that I have created have progressively gotten better. The first was the standing red mermaid in which I was inspired by the little mermaid and the biopic film Selena was not executed as well as the other three projects. Each poster I gained skillful knowledge to incorporate a cleaner design. When I design I like simplicity with a visual that catches the eye and that is what was in mind behind these projects. My favorite, the Design Career Fair poster came out exactly how I sketched it and took the most time. Each element in the thought bubble was hand picked cropped and warped to fit the look that I wanted, and it came out fantastic.
