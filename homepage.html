The Sewing Machine

The sewing machine is one of the most important machines of the last three centuries. It revolutionized the garment and fashion industry by allowing mass productions of clothing. With the emergence of the sewing machine it pushed hand sewers out of popular demand and made way for factories and sweat shops. It also took fashion from couture and high fashion to readymade and easily accessed. Today the sewing machine is a part of commercial use but is seen as skill that many are not in tune with.
